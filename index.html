Recipe of the Day: Mantu (Afghan Dumplings)

Ingredients
Ground meat (beef or lamb)
Onions
Dough
Garlic
Spices (salt, pepper, cumin)
Yogurt (for serving)

Instructions:

  • Prepare the dough and let it rest.
  • Mix ground meat with chopped onions, garlic, and spices.
  • Stuff the dough with the meat mixture and shape into dumplings.
  • Steam the dumplings for about 40 minutes.
  • Serve with yogurt on the side.
